Why Take This Course?

What is the purpose of learning HTML?

In the state of the world we live in, the ability to understand and utilize multiple languages is a critical skill for any person to learn. However, the utility of being multilingual isn't exclusive to spoken words. Understanding coding languages is as phenomenal a skill for an individual to learn as learning alternate methods of communication. With technology being such a prominent aspect of the modern day, knowing how to work with it is a major way to take steps forward.
